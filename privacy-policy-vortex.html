Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Vortex Emulator ("the App") is developed by JosuSM. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Data Collection

Vortex Emulator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App runs entirely on your device.

  • No accounts or registration are required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No personal information is collected, shared, or sold.

2. Local Storage

The App stores data locally on your device for functionality purposes:

  • ROM files you choose to open (read-only access).
  • Save states and battery saves for your games.
  • App preferences (display settings, control layouts).
  • Emulation core libraries downloaded from official libretro repositories.

All of this data stays on your device and is never transmitted externally.

3. Storage Permissions

The App may request storage permissions to access ROM files stored on your device. This access is used solely to load game files you select. No files are uploaded, shared, or accessed beyond your explicit selection.

4. Internet Access

The App uses internet access for:

  • Downloading emulation core libraries from official open-source repositories.
  • Optional multiplayer features (LAN and internet netplay), where only game input data is exchanged between connected players during a session.

No personal data is transmitted during these operations.

5. Multiplayer

When using the multiplayer feature, the App exchanges game controller input data with other connected players in real-time. No personal information, device identifiers, or location data is shared. Multiplayer sessions are temporary and no data is retained after disconnection.

6. Third-Party Services

Vortex Emulator does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data (no ads, no analytics, no crash reporting services).

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. As the App collects no personal data from any user, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
